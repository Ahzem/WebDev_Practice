How to Make a Sandwich...

  • Initial Steps:
    1. Wash your hands
    2. Take out two slices of bread
    3. Spread mayonnaise on one slice
  • Next 02 Steps:
    1. Add lettuce leaves
    2. Place a slice of turkey
  • Next 02 Steps:
    1. Add a slice of cheese
    2. Put the other slice of bread on top
  • Final Steps:
    1. Cut the sandwich into halves
    2. Serve and enjoy!
